From Cake to Create üç∞

My passion for design began during a high school business project. My team and I started a small business selling banana cake, and I took charge of designing the logo, packaging, and website, using Wix at the time. That was when I discovered the joy of bringing ideas to life visually, and I realized how powerful design can be in shaping how people experience something, even as simple as a piece of cake.

Although I spent most of my early life in Vietnam, that experience gave me the confidence and motivation to pursue design more seriously. Five years ago, I made the big decision to move to Canada and begin my academic journey in design. It's been a challenging but incredibly rewarding path, and I'm grateful for how far that one high school project has taken me.

Outside of my design life,

...you'll often find me out and about in the springtime, phone in hand, chasing the perfect shot of cherry blossoms in full bloom. There's something magical about those pink petals that always clams me down and makes me happy. And when I'm not out photographing it, I'm probably cozied up somewhere, sketching on my iPad, or getting lost in a good game.
